About Umuhanzi

A group founded by Ishimwe Bertrand in order to support Rwandan singers to put their arts on international level but also making Rwandans love their unique culture and be proud of it.

Our Mission: To promote Rwandan arts in order to be recognised on international level.
Our vision: To be the first country in Africa with recognisable and influencing cultural heritage.

About show me
what you got festival tour

In order to support Rwandan artists, Umuhanzi group has launched show me what you got festival tour which will give those artists the opportunity to perform live in front of their fans, and observe how people love their songs.
For the people who will be attending, they will enjoy the quality of Rwandan music, sing and dance together with their favorite stars. These festivals will be taking place in various places across the country.
Let us support our artists by loving and supporting their arts.
