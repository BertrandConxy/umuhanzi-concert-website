Let's show love to our music

Show me
what you got music festival tour

In order to support Rwandan singers, umuhanzi group has launched show me what you got music festival tour which will give singers the opportunity to perform live in front of their fans and be able to observe how much people love their songs.

Let's support our artists by loving and supporting their arts.

These festivals will be taking place in various places across the country of thousand hills and on different dates. More details are below in the following sections.
