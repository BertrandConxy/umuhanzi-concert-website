Show me what you got music festival tour

In order to support Rwandan singers, umuhanzi group has launched show me what you got music festival tourwhich will give singers the opportunity to perform live in front of their fans and be able to observe how much people love their songs. For the people who will be attending, they will be enjoying the quality of rwandan music, sing and dance together with their favorite stars. They will have time to take selfies with their favorite stars!

Let's support our artists by loving and supporting their arts
